Biography

Joe Canuel was born on April 25, 1992. He has lived in Peabody, Massachusetts his entire life and graduated from Peabody Veterans Memorial High School in 2010. He is the oldest of five brothers, and comes from a family-oriented background. Joe is interested in the fields of Computer Science and Mathematics, but is unsure of what career in these fields he wants to pursue. In the Fall of 2010 he enrolled at Tufts University. He is enrolled in the school of Engineering, and is pursuing a double major in Computer Science and Mathematics.
